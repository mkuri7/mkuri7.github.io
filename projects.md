# Projects

## LotoLab AI

AI-assisted lottery data analysis platform for Japanese Loto 6 and Loto 7.

LotoLab AI is not designed to predict winning numbers.  
Its purpose is to explore lottery data through statistics, machine learning, simulation, and reproducible software engineering practices.
